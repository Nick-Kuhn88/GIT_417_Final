About

RENEE KATHLEEN KOHER is originally from Chicago, IL and received her BFA in Musical Theater from the Hartt School of Music in Connecticut. Renee has been teaching voice and acting for over 20 years. She moved to the valley 6 years ago and started RKK voice and Acting Studio, where she has had the pleasure of training so many incredible performers across the country. Renee also has directed multiple youth productions as well as performed all over the world. Please follow her on Social Media at RKK Voice and Acting Studio.
